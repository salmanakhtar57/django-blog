Rock Paper Scissors in Python

A Step-by-Step Guide to implement ‘Rock Paper Scissors’ in Python

Rock Paper Scissors is a fun game, played between two players. Each player picks one option either rock, paper, or scissors. And the one who picks the right option wins. The winner is determined by three simple rules. Here are the rules of the game: Rock beats Scissors Paper beats Rock Scissors beat paper. So according to the rules, if you choose rock, and the other player chooses scissors, or you choose paper, and the other player chooses rock, or you choose scissors, and the other player chooses paper, then you will win the game. But if the opposite is the case then the other player wins. The match will be drawn if both choose the same option.
